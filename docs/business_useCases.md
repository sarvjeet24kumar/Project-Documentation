#  Business Use Cases

## Overview
Business use cases represent the **organizational goals** and **value-added processes** achieved through CampusConnect.  
They focus on *why* the system exists and *how* it improves college operations and engagement.

---

##  Use Case 1: Streamlining Campus Communication
**Goal:** Centralize communication between students, clubs, and mentors.  
**Business Value:** 
 Eliminates fragmented WhatsApp or noticeboard updates.
 Ensures all event announcements are available in one place.

---

##  Use Case 2: Empowering Student Participation
**Goal:** Increase participation in extracurricular activities.  
**Business Value:**
    Tracks attendance and engagement data.
    Encourages student involvement through visibility and ease of registration.

---


##  Use Case 3: Simplifying  Administration
**Goal:** Reduce manual management for club coordinators.  
**Business Value:**
 Admins can easily manage events, participants, and schedules.
 Decreases paperwork and duplicate data entry.
